Printable bakery menus by Canva

Aside from making scrumptious cakes, bread, and pastries, one of the most important tasks when selling your baked goods is creating menus. After all, if you're selling a bunch of your intricate creations, you need to have a vehicle in which you can collate your offerings and have potential customers choose. You can entice them to buy your goods by creating a stunning menu worthy of your baked products, and you can do that with a breeze with Canva's bakery menu templates.

Designing cakes is one thing, but designing menus is a whole other challenge. You don't have to worry about having prior graphic design experience with our proprietary editing tool that allows you to put your creative juices to work. Select a bakery menu design from the massive library of templates, ideally one that best fits your store. You can then jump right ahead and blow your competition out of the water by customizing your bakery menu card template to your heart's content. Make sure to add photos of your best work to your menu. Easily arrange your pics with grids and frames. Then, have a look through our impressive media library filled with millions of images, illustrations, icons, vectors, and other design elements to help you represent your brand.

Whether you go for pastel, cartoonish, or whimsical, you can think of your bakery menu as a work of art. With Canva’s drag-and-drop tools, hiring a professional designer won’t be necessary. You can focus on more important aspects of your bakery while we take care of your marketing materials!

Once your design looks as good as your baked goods, you have several choices for spreading the word. Download your design as a high-resolution PDF file for printing, or, click the print button to use Canva Print’s professional printing service. We’ll make sure to produce menus to be proud of. You can also post your design to your social media accounts to pique your customers’ interest and even attract new ones!
